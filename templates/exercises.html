Simple Exercises

Abs

Stretches listed in the other routine section. For every 5 minutes do 2 sets of the following

  • 10 narrow squats
  • bicycle 2 minutes
  • 6 V-ups
  • plank 2 minutes
  • scissor kicks 30 sec

For every 15 minutes do 4 sets of the following

  • 5 narrow squats
  • bicycle 2 minute
  • plank 1 minute
  • 10 V-ups

5 minutes do 2 sets of the following

  • bicycle 1 minute
  • 5 V8s
  • plank 1 minute
  • scissor kicks 15 sec

Cardio

For every 15 minutes do 2 sets of the following

  • 30 jumping jacks
  • 30 push-ups
  • 30 sit-ups
  • jog in place 30 sec

For every 15 minutes do 6 sets of the following

  • 25 jumping jacks
  • 25 push-ups
  • 25 sit-ups
  • jog in place 25 sec

5 minutes do 2 sets of the following

  • 15 jumping jacks
  • 15 push-ups
  • 15 sit-ups
  • jog in place 15 sec
