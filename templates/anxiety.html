Anxiety

Things to try:

  • Take naps. A 10-20 minute nap gives you mental alertness and lets you feel rejuvenated
  • Think of things to be thankful for. You might feel scared or anxious of what will happen next, but if you realize what you have and how much you have, you will feel more relieved.
  • Question why you feel anxious. Is the worry realistic? What would make you feel better about what you are worried about.
  • Drink water. studies have shown that dehydration can cause the bravest of people to worry about the simplest things. Drinking water will make you feel better

Meditation:

  • When you are scared of something or worry try this breathing technique.
  • Breathe in for seconds
  • Hold for two seconds,
  • And breathe out for 4 seconds

  • Think of this as you do it
  • In one, two
  • Hold one, two
  • Out two, three, four
