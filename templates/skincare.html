Skincare Practices

  • This is best done after showering
  • Rinse your face to first get of dirt
  • (Optional) put on a face mask and follow the mask directions
  • (Once a week only) use face scrub to scrub any germs under your pores. concentrate this on your cheeks, nose, and chin.
  • Dab your face lightly until your face is damp, but not dry.
  • apply face moisturizer(make sure there are excessive amounts on your cheek and in the crevice around your nose).
  • Keep in mind that only the face rinsing is necessary if you are doing a full coverage makeup look using primer.
